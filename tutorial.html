About Our University

Today Yerevan «Haybusak» University is a unique internationally oriented educational scientific center that is widely known not only in Armenia but also in a number of foreign states.

Yerevan «Haybusak» University was one of the first ones that passed verification by the Quality of Education and received the state accreditation by all functioning specialties that was an evidence of high trust of the State and a guarantee of successful development of the University as «Haybusak» was entitled with the right to award state diplomas to its graduates. «Haybusak» was entitled with the right to award state diplomas to its graduates.
